Our Team

Each person found their way on this project through a combination of hustle and serendipity. We have a combination of expertise in education, Fine Art, and Science build better apps for children. As a team, we plan, strategize and imagine a new experience for children learning to code. Our diversity is our strength. We represent the best of design and engineering for fun, playful and educational adventure for children.

Our Vision

We are reimagining how children learn to code by creating a playground at the intersection of art, design and technology.

We understand how children learn

  • Early Childhood Education Research based design
  • Children can figure things out without feeling stuck
  • Innovative, open-ended coding environment that encourages experimentation

We value art, creativity and play

  • Colorful, imaginative creating
  • Visually intuitive learning experience
  • Fun and engaging coding adventure

We believe everyone is an engineer at heart

  • Immersive engineering at play for children
  • Designed by kids at heart for all kids
  • Parents can be confident their children are learning they are engineers too!
